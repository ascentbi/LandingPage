Frequently Asked Questions

01. Why Power BI instead of Excel?

Excel is manual and error-prone. Power BI automates data collection and ensures your team looks at one "single source of truth."

02. Do I need to pay for the 450€ or 1.250€ plan every time I want a new report?

Not necessarily. The Growth Engine plan builds a robust "Centralized Data Model." Once that engine is built, creating additional reports is much faster and cheaper. Many clients start with a major project to build the foundation and then use the monthly Data Partner plan to add new views as needed.

03. How much of my time will this take? I'm already too busy.

My goal is to save you time, not take it. We typically start with one 30-minute discovery call to identify your KPIs and get data access. From there, I handle the heavy lifting. You’ll only need to spend about 15 minutes mid-way through for a prototype review to ensure the logic matches your business reality.
